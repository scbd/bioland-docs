## Site Manager Role

Site Managers have full access to menu management with additional capabilities. This section covers all menu operations available to Site Managers.

### Add Menu - Site Manager {#site-manager-add-steps}

Site Managers can add new menus with advanced settings. Here are the steps.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Step 1: Open the Admin Area

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-01-admin-menu.png -->

1. Log in to your site
2. Click your name at the top right
3. Click "Administration"

#### Step 2: Go to Structure

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-02-structure-menu.png -->

1. Look in the left menu
2. Find "Structure"
3. Click "Structure"

#### Step 3: Find Menus

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-03-menus-list.png -->

1. Look for "Menus" in the Structure section
2. Click "Menus"
3. You will see a list of all menus

#### Step 4: Start Adding a New Menu

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-04-add-button.png -->

1. Look at the top of the page
2. Find the "+ Add menu" button
3. Click the button

#### Step 5: Fill in Menu Details

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-05-menu-form.png -->

1. Type the menu name (like "Main Menu")
2. Add a description (optional)
3. This helps you remember what the menu is for

#### Step 6: Configure Menu Settings

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-06-menu-settings.png -->

1. Choose where the menu appears
2. Set the menu language
3. Configure other options if needed

#### Step 7: Review Advanced Options

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-07-advanced-settings.png -->

1. Site Managers see extra settings
2. Configure permissions if needed
3. Set visibility rules (optional)

#### Step 8: Save the Menu

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-08-save-button.png -->

1. Check all your information
2. Find the "Save" button at the bottom
3. Click "Save"

#### Step 9: See Your New Menu

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-add-site-manager-09-success-message.png -->

1. You will see a success message
2. Your new menu appears in the list
3. Now you can add menu items to it

---

### Edit Menu - Site Manager {#site-manager-edit-steps}

Site Managers can edit all menu settings, including advanced options. Here are the steps.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### What You Can Change

- Menu name
- Menu description
- Where the menu appears
- Menu permissions
- Menu visibility rules

#### Step 1: Open the Admin Area

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-01-admin-menu.png -->

1. Log in to your site
2. Click your name at the top right
3. Click "Administration"

#### Step 2: Go to Structure

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-02-structure-menu.png -->

1. Look in the left menu
2. Find "Structure"
3. Click "Structure"

#### Step 3: Find the Menu List

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-03-menus-list.png -->

1. Click "Menus" in the Structure section
2. Find the menu you want to change
3. Look at the list of all menus

#### Step 4: Click Edit

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-04-edit-button.png -->

1. Find your menu in the list
2. Click the "Edit" button next to it
3. The edit form opens

#### Step 5: Make Basic Changes

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-05-edit-form.png -->

1. Change the menu name if needed
2. Update the description
3. Modify basic settings

#### Step 6: Configure Advanced Settings

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-06-advanced-settings.png -->

1. Site Managers see extra options
2. Change permissions if needed
3. Update visibility rules

#### Step 7: Review All Changes

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-07-review-changes.png -->

1. Check all your changes
2. Make sure everything is correct
3. Verify advanced settings

#### Step 8: Save Your Changes

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-08-save-button.png -->

1. Find the "Save" button
2. Click "Save"
3. Your changes are saved

#### Step 9: See the Updated Menu

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-edit-site-manager-09-success-message.png -->

1. You see a success message
2. The menu list shows your changes
3. Your menu is now updated

### Delete Menu - Site Manager {#site-manager-delete-steps}

Site Managers can delete menus with additional confirmation steps. Here are the steps.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Before You Delete

- Make sure no pages use this menu
- Check if the menu appears on your website
- Save a backup if you might need it later

#### Step 1: Open the Admin Area

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-01-admin-menu.png -->

1. Log in to your site
2. Click your name at the top right
3. Click "Administration"

#### Step 2: Go to Structure

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-02-structure-menu.png -->

1. Look in the left menu
2. Find "Structure"
3. Click "Structure"

#### Step 3: Find the Menu List

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-03-menus-list.png -->

1. Click "Menus" in the Structure section
2. Find the menu you want to delete
3. Look at the list of all menus

#### Step 4: Click Delete

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-04-delete-button.png -->

1. Find your menu in the list
2. Click the "Delete" button next to it
3. A warning appears

#### Step 5: Review Impact

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-05-impact-warning.png -->

1. Site Managers see extra warnings
2. See which pages use this menu
3. Check the impact of deletion

#### Step 6: Confirm Deletion

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-06-confirm-delete.png -->

1. Read all warnings carefully
2. Make sure you want to delete
3. Click "Delete" to confirm

#### Step 7: See Deletion Success

<!-- SCREENSHOT NEEDED: /images/biosafety-land/menus/menu-delete-site-manager-07-success-message.png -->

1. You see a success message
2. The menu is gone from the list
3. The menu is permanently deleted
