## Contributor Role

Contributors can add new content to the site. This section covers all content operations available to Contributors.

### Add Content - Contributor {#add-content-contributor}

Contributors can add new content to the site. Here are the steps.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Step 1: Open the Admin Toolbar

1. Look at the top of the page
2. Find the black admin toolbar
3. Locate the **Publishing** menu

![Admin Toolbar](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-02-admin-menu.png)

#### Step 2: Navigate to Content

1. Click **Publishing** in the toolbar
2. A dropdown menu will appear
3. Click on **Content**

![Publishing Menu](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-03-navigate-content.png)

#### Step 3: Click Add Content Button

1. You will see a list of existing content
2. Look for the **Add Content** button at the top
3. Click **Add Content**

![Content List with Add Button](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-04-add-button.png)

#### Step 4: Choose Content Type

1. A new page will load showing available content types
2. You will see different content types listed
3. Click on the content type you want to create

Common content types:

- **Article/News** - For news and updates
- **Page** - For general information
- **Document** - For files and downloads
- **Event** - For meetings and events

For a complete list of content types and their fields, see [Content Types](/biosafety-land/context/content-types).

![Content Type Selection](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-05-choose-type.png)

#### Step 5: Fill In the Content Form

1. Enter a **Title** for your content
2. Fill in the **Body** text using the rich text editor
   - You can format text (bold, italic, lists)
   - Add links and images inline
   - Write meaningful paragraphs
3. Complete any other required fields marked with an asterisk (*)

![Empty Content Form](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-03-create-form.png)

::: tip Writing Good Content
Write clear, descriptive paragraphs. Use headings to break up long content. The body editor supports rich formatting - see [Body Editor Guide](./body-editor) for details.
:::

![Form with Content](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-05-form-filled.png)

#### Step 6: Complete Additional Fields

1. Scroll down to see additional fields
2. Add images, attachments, or media if applicable
3. Select categories or tags
4. Fill in any other optional fields relevant to your content type

![Additional Fields](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-06-fill-form.png)

#### Step 7: Review Publishing Options

::: warning Contributor Limitation
As a Contributor, you **cannot publish content directly**. Your content will be saved as a draft for review.
:::

1. Scroll down to the publishing section
2. Note that the "Published" checkbox may be disabled or hidden for Contributors
3. Your content will be saved as **Unpublished/Draft** by default

![Publishing Options (Limited for Contributor)](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-07-publish-options.png)

#### Step 8: Save Your Content

1. Review all the information you entered
2. Click **Save** at the bottom of the form
3. Wait for the success message
4. Your content is now saved as a draft!

![Save Button](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-add-contributor-08-save.png)

#### What Happens Next?

::: info Contributor Workflow
As a Contributor, your content is saved as a **draft** and needs to be reviewed before publishing.
:::

1. Your content is saved but **not yet published**
2. **Notify a Content Manager or Site Manager** to review your content
3. They will review the content and publish it when ready
4. You can continue to edit your draft until it is published
5. You can find your content in the content list with status "Unpublished"

### Edit Content - Contributor {#edit-content-contributor}

Contributors can edit content they created. Here are the steps.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Step 1: Go to Content List

1. Click **Publishing** in the toolbar
2. Click **Content** from the dropdown
3. You will see a list of content

![Content List](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-edit-contributor-01-content-list.png)

#### Step 2: Find Content and Locate Edit Button

1. Look through the content list for your content
2. You can use the search box or filters
3. Find the **Edit** button in the Operations column on the right
4. The Edit button may be in a dropdown - click the arrow to see options

![Content List with Edit Button](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-edit-contributor-02-unpublished-list.png)

#### Step 3: Open the Edit Form

1. Click **Edit** on your content
2. The content edit form will load
3. You will see the current content ready for editing

![Edit Form](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-edit-contributor-03-edit-form.png)

#### Step 4: Update the Content

1. Modify the title if needed
2. Edit the body text - add, remove, or change content
3. Use the editor toolbar to format text
4. Update any other fields as needed
5. Add or remove images and attachments

::: tip Editing Best Practice
Make meaningful edits. Add a paragraph to expand the content, correct errors, or update outdated information.
:::

![Editing Content in Body](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-edit-contributor-04-editing-changes.png)

#### Step 5: Add Revision Note

1. Scroll to the bottom
2. Find the **Revision Log** section
3. Enter a note about what you changed
4. This helps track changes over time

Example notes:

- "Fixed spelling errors"
- "Added new images"
- "Updated contact information"

![Revision Log](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-edit-contributor-05-edit-changes.png)

#### Step 6: Save Your Changes

1. Review all changes you made
2. Click **Save** at the bottom
3. Wait for the success message
4. Your changes are now saved!

![Save Button](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-edit-contributor-06-save-success.png)

#### What Happens Next?

::: info For Contributors
Editing does not change the publication status. If content was unpublished, it remains unpublished after editing.
:::

- Your changes are saved as a new revision
- A new revision is created in the revision history
- If the content was **already published**, changes are visible immediately
- If the content was **unpublished (draft)**, it remains a draft
- To publish draft content, notify a Content Manager or Site Manager
- You can edit the content again at any time

### Delete Content - Contributor {#delete-content-contributor}

Contributors can delete content they created. Here are the steps.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Step 1: Go to Content List

1. Click **Publishing** in the toolbar
2. Click **Content** from the dropdown
3. You will see a list of content

![Content List](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-delete-contributor-01-content-list.png)

#### Step 2: Find Content to Delete

1. Look through the content list
2. You can only delete content you created
3. Use the search box to find it quickly
4. Use filters if needed

![Find Content](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-delete-contributor-02-actions-menu.png)

#### Step 3: Click Delete Button

1. Find your content in the list
2. Look for the **Delete** button on the right
3. Click **Delete**

![Click Delete](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-delete-contributor-02-select-delete.png)

#### Step 4: Confirm Deletion

1. A confirmation message will appear
2. Read the warning carefully
3. Make sure you want to delete this content
4. Remember: You cannot undo this action

![Confirm Delete](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-delete-contributor-03-confirm-dialog.png)

#### Step 5: Complete the Deletion

1. Click **Delete** to confirm
2. Or click **Cancel** to keep the content
3. Wait for the success message
4. The content is now deleted

![Complete Delete](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-delete-contributor-04-success-message.png)

#### What Happens Next?

- The content is removed permanently
- It no longer appears in the content list
- It cannot be recovered
- Any links to it will break
- Consider archiving instead if you might need it later

#### Important Notes

⚠️ **Warning:**

- Deletion is permanent
- You cannot undo this action
- Make sure you have a backup if needed
- Check if other content links to this page

✅ **Before Deleting:**

- Check if content is linked elsewhere
- Download important information
- Tell your team if needed
- Consider unpublishing instead of deleting

### Clone Content - Contributor {#clone-content-contributor}

Contributors can clone existing content to make a copy. This helps when you want to create similar content.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Step 1: Go to Content List

1. Click **Publishing** in the toolbar
2. Click **Content** from the dropdown
3. You will see a list of content

![Content List](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-01-content-list.png)

#### Step 2: Find the Content to Clone

1. Look through the content list
2. Find the content you want to copy
3. You can use the search box to find it faster

![Select Content](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-03-clone-option.png)

#### Step 3: Open Clone Options

1. Click on the content row
2. Look for the **Operations** dropdown
3. Click on **Operations**
4. A menu will appear

![Choose Clone](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-04-clone-form.png)

#### Step 4: Select Clone Option

1. In the operations menu, find **Clone**
2. Click on **Clone**
3. A new page will load

![New Title](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-05-edit-title.png)

#### Step 5: Review Clone Settings

1. The system copies all content fields
2. The title will have "(Clone)" added
3. Review the copied information
4. Make any changes you need

![Clone Settings](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-06-save.png)

#### Step 6: Edit Title and Content

1. Change the title to remove "(Clone)"
2. Give it a new title
3. Update any content that needs to change
4. Keep what you want from the original

![Save Clone](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-07-success.png)

#### Step 7: Save Your Clone

1. Scroll to the bottom of the form
2. Click the **Save** button
3. Wait for the success message
4. Your cloned content is now saved

![Verify Clone](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-clone-contributor-08-cloned-content.png)

#### What Contributors Can and Cannot Do

**Can do:**
- Clone any published content (own or others')
- Clone own unpublished (draft) content

**Cannot do:**
- Cannot change clone permissions
- Cannot bulk clone multiple items
- Cannot change author on cloned content

### Translate Content - Contributor {#translate-content-contributor}

Contributors can translate content they created. This helps make content available in different languages.

::: details Authenticated?
If you have not already logged in, follow the [Login](/biosafety-land/authentication/login) instructions.
:::

#### Step 1: Go to Content List

1. Click **Publishing** in the toolbar
2. Click **Content** from the dropdown
3. You will see a list of content

![Content List](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-01-translations-list.png)

#### Step 2: Find Content to Translate

1. Look through the content list
2. Find content you want to translate
3. You can only translate your own content
4. Use the search box to find it faster

![Select Content](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-03-translations-page.png)

#### Step 3: Open Translate Options

1. Click on the content row
2. Look for the **Translate** button
3. Click on **Translate**
4. A new page will load

![Translate Option](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-04-add-translation.png)

#### Step 4: Choose Target Language

1. You will see a list of languages
2. Find the language you want to translate to
3. Click **Add** next to that language
4. The translation form will open

![Select Language](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-05-translation-form.png)

#### Step 5: Fill in Translation

1. The original content shows on the left
2. Enter translated content on the right
3. Translate the title first
4. Translate the body text
5. Translate all other fields

![Translate Content](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-06-translated-content.png)

#### Step 6: Review Your Translation

1. Check your translated text
2. Make sure nothing is missing
3. Verify special terms are correct
4. Check that formatting looks good

![Save Translation](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-07-save.png)

#### Step 7: Save the Translation

1. Scroll to the bottom
2. Click **Save** or **Save and Publish**
3. Wait for the success message
4. Your translation is now saved!

![Verify Translation](/images/biosafety-land/content/content-translate-contributor-08-success.png)

#### What Contributors Cannot Do

- Cannot translate others' content
- Cannot change translation settings
- Cannot manage language configurations
- Cannot delete translations without deleting original
