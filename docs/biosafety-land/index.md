## What is Biosafety Land?

Biosafety Land is the national-level platform for the Biosafety Clearing-House (BCH), supporting implementation of the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety. It enables countries to:

- **Manage biosafety information** - Document LMOs, field trials, and regulatory decisions
- **Publish content** - Share announcements, documents, and national information
- **Organize events** - Coordinate meetings, workshops, and capacity-building activities
- **Maintain records** - Keep comprehensive biosafety databases and documentation
