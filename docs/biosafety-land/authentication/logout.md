# Logout

This guide explains how to safely log out of the Biosafety Land system.

## How to Log Out

1. Click on your **user profile** icon or name in the top right of the admin toolbar
2. A dropdown menu will appear
3. Click **Log out** from the dropdown menu

![User Menu with Logout Option](/images/biosafety-land/authentication/logout-01-user-menu-location.png)

## What Happens When You Log Out

- Your session is terminated
- Any unsaved changes may be lost
- You are redirected to the login page or home page

::: warning
Always save your work before logging out to prevent data loss.
:::

## Session Expiration

For security purposes, your session will automatically expire after a period of inactivity. When this happens:

- You will be redirected to the login page
- Any unsaved work may be lost
- You will need to log in again to continue

## Security Recommendations

### Always Log Out When:

- Using a shared or public computer
- Stepping away from your workstation
- Finished working for the day

### Additional Security Tips

- Close your browser after logging out on shared devices
- Clear browser history and cookies on public computers
- Never leave your account logged in unattended
